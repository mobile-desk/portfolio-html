Our Journey

Founded in 2020 by Praise Obasi, TechInnovom emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions. As a female Nigerian Python developer, Praise recognized the untapped potential in combining technical excellence with innovative problem-solving approaches.

Starting with a small team of dedicated developers, TechInnovom quickly gained recognition for its expertise in Python development and data analysis. Our commitment to quality and innovation attracted top talent from across the globe, allowing us to expand our services and impact.

Today, we're proud to have grown into a diverse team of professionals, serving clients worldwide. Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, adaptation, and a steadfast commitment to pushing technological boundaries while maintaining our core values of excellence, innovation, and integrity.
